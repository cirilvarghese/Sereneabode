Price List

Basic Plan

5%
of the Monthly Rent
Services Included
  • Rent Collection & Disbursement:
    Efficient collection of rent from tenants and timely transfer to landlords.
  • Tenant Communication:
    Handling routine communications, addressing tenant inquiries, and resolving minor issues.
  • Lease Enforcement:
    Ensuring compliance with lease terms and managing any violations appropriately.
  • Financial Reporting:
    Providing monthly statements detailing income and expenses for transparency.
  • Maintenance Coordination:
    Arranging necessary repairs and maintenance to uphold property standards.
  • Tenant Screening & Selection:
    Conducting background checks to secure reliable tenants.

Standard Plan

6%
of the Monthly Rent
Includes All Basic Plan Services
  • Property Marketing & Advertising:
    Promoting vacant properties through various channels to attract quality tenants.
  • Lease Preparation & Execution:
    Drafting and managing lease agreements to ensure legal compliance.
  • Move-In/Move-Out Inspections:
    Conducting inspections to document property conditions before and after tenancy.
  • Eviction Coordination:
    Managing the eviction process in compliance with legal requirements, if necessary.

Premium Plan

7%
of the Monthly Rent
Includes All Standard Plan Services
  • 24/7 Emergency Maintenance Support:
    Providing round-the-clock assistance for urgent maintenance issues.
  • Regular Property Inspections:
    Conducting scheduled inspections to proactively identify and address potential issues.
  • Vendor Management:
    Coordinating with trusted service providers for repairs and maintenance.
  • Tenant Placement Guarantee:
    Offering assurance on tenant quality, with provisions for replacement if necessary.
New Tenant Placement Fee:

A one-time fee equivalent to 50% of one month's rent for securing new tenants.

This fee covers:
  • Advertising and marketing expenses.
  • Comprehensive tenant screening processes.
  • Preparation of lease agreements.
